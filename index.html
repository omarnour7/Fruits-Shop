Orange

Oranges are rich in Vitamin C, which boosts your immune system and helps in collagen production for healthy skin. One orange can provide over 100% of your daily recommended Vitamin C intake!

Grapes

Grapes contain powerful antioxidants like resveratrol, which may help protect against heart disease and support brain health. Grapes are also a great source of hydration since they are made of about 82% water!

Guava

Guavas have 4x more Vitamin C than oranges and are rich in dietary fiber, making them excellent for digestion and immunity. One guava can give you more than 200% of your daily Vitamin C needs!
